About me

My passion for technology started very early. At just 8 years old, I built my very first website out of pure curiosity. That simple project was the spark. At 9, I dove into Unity to understand how to create games. A year later, I was building my own version of Flappy Bird, discovering both the joy (and frustration) of game development.

As I grew older, I wanted to go further. I learned how to work with databases and then moved into web development with Flask and Django. These frameworks opened the door to Python, a language I now use almost daily. Always looking to push my limits, I jumped into deep learning. Understanding neural networks, working with datasets, training models... it quickly stopped being just a hobby. It became a true passion.

Today, I continue learning and developing projects that combine my core interests: artificial intelligence, web development, and interactive systems. I love turning ideas into real solutions, exploring cutting-edge technologies, and—most of all—building with purpose.
